About MSTA....

The Maruti Suzuki Training Academy (MSTA) is a comprehensive initiative by Maruti Suzuki India Limited, aimed at enhancing the skills and competencies of its employees, dealers, vendors, and partners. The academy focuses on various aspects of talent development, including behavioral, technical, and functional skills.

Vision: MSTA’s vision is to drive business excellence by establishing a talent management system framework through competency-based development across the value chain and community1.

Objectives: The objectives include establishing an academy of excellence to meet specific business needs and capability

Programs: MSTA offers various programs such as skill development initiatives with the Government of India, vocational training under the Skill Development Initiative (SDI), and courses in Automotive Repair under the Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS)1.

Technology & Infrastructure: They leverage technology for training, including a virtual classroom for remote delivery and a Learning Management System for self-paced learning1.

Student Recognition & Awards: MSTA students have achieved recognition and awards in various skills competitions, reflecting the quality of training provided1
